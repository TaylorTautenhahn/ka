Product Overview

One system for evaluating, coordinating, deciding, and preserving recruitment operations.

BidBoard is structured around the real work of rush week: capture better signal, coordinate ownership, walk into meetings prepared, and protect the season after the vote.

Evaluate live Coordinate next steps Decide with context Preserve the season

Outcome Modules

Organized around the outcomes leadership actually needs.

Evaluate

Collect better signal without slowing the room down.

  • Per-rushee profiles with photos, contact details, notes, and tags
  • Weighted rating engine with update comments and stored deltas
  • Role-aware visibility for heads, officers, and members

Coordinate

Make ownership obvious across lunches, events, and follow-up.

  • Assignments, package links, and shared touchpoint ownership
  • Unified rush calendar plus lunch scheduling and export links
  • Officer chat, goals, and task tracking in one workspace

Decide

Walk into meetings with the context already assembled.

  • Meeting packets with rating trends and linked recent activity
  • Package partner context, lunch notes, and quick comparison flow
  • Decision-ready summaries instead of memory-driven debate

Preserve

Keep the season durable before, during, and after deployment.

  • Persistent storage validation and admin diagnostics
  • CSV exports, DB backups, and season archive/reset
  • Multi-organization separation with independent branding

Operating Surfaces

Each workspace has a clear job in the rush workflow.

Command

Priority queue, rating-first execution, recent changes, and live follow-up in one daily operating surface.

Rushees

Roster management, filters, package linking, photos, assignments, and contact export from one detailed view.

Operations

Calendar, lunches, goals, notifications, and officer coordination without switching tools.

Meetings

Packet queue, shortlist review, trend context, and PDF-ready detail when chapter discussion begins.
