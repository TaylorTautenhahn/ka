Product Overview

One system for evaluating, coordinating, deciding, and preserving recruitment operations.

BidBoard is built to keep the whole rush cycle coherent: collect better signal, coordinate faster, walk into meetings prepared, and protect the season after the decision is made.

Evaluate live Coordinate outreach Decide with context Preserve the season

Outcome Modules

Organized around what recruitment teams actually need to get done.

Evaluate

Capture better signal without slowing the room down.

  • Per-rushee profiles with notes, contact details, and photos
  • Weighted rating engine with update comments and deltas
  • Role-aware visibility for officers, heads, and members

Coordinate

Make ownership obvious across lunches, events, and follow-up.

  • Assignments and package-link visibility
  • Unified rush calendar and lunch scheduling
  • Officer chat and task tracking in one workspace

Decide

Walk into meeting with context already assembled.

  • Dedicated meeting packets with trend history
  • Linked profiles, notes, and recent changes
  • Decision-ready summaries instead of memory-driven debate

Preserve

Keep the season durable before, during, and after deployment.

  • Persistent storage validation and diagnostics
  • CSV exports, DB backups, and season archive/reset
  • Multi-organization separation with independent branding

Operating Surfaces

Every page has a clear job in the rush workflow.

Dashboard

Command center for priority ratings, recent changes, stale follow-up, and quick lunch scheduling.

Rushees

Full-detail roster management, filters, package linking, photos, and assignment ownership.

Team + Calendar

See who owns what, who is behind, and how rush week activity is unfolding across the chapter.

Meeting Packets

Open a focused review surface with ratings, lunches, notes, and trend movement in one page.
