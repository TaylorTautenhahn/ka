Product Overview

The recruitment operating system for modern chapters.

BidBoard unifies scoring, accountability, member coordination, and season continuity for fraternities and sororities running serious recruitment operations.

Core Modules

Every workflow connected to one data model

Identity + Access

Per-user accounts, role permissions, approvals/disapprovals, and auditable session controls.

Rushee Intelligence

Profiles with photos, contact details, assignment ownership, notes, and interaction history.

Weighted Rating Engine

Custom rubric scoring with update deltas, comment requirements, and role-based weighting logic.

Meeting Command View

Dedicated packet page with trend graph, live context, and PDF export for formal deliberations.

Engagement Tracking

Lunch scheduling, shared calendar feed, and member-level participation metrics in real time.

Data Portability

CSV backup bundles, DB snapshots, Google Form imports, and season archive snapshots.

Operational Flow

How teams run recruitment with BidBoard

Capture + Enrich

Import forms, add rushees, assign officers, and attach photos and notes from day one.

Evaluate + Coordinate

Rate each rushee, review deltas, schedule lunches, and monitor leaderboards and matching views.

Decide + Archive

Use meeting packets to decide bids, then archive the season with confirmation safeguards.
